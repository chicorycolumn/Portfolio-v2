Back in 2019 my love for puzzles had me making cryptic crosswords. I searched for data-crunching tools to level-up, but found none to my satisfaction. Having adored the Symbol Logic electives on my Philosophy degree, I decided to teach myself coding to build a crossword maker.

This later led me to Northcoders, an award-winning three month fullstack JavaScript course. I enjoyed the keen focus on pair-programming, teamwork, plus encouragement of individual initiative. Our final project used daily standups, kanban boards, and an Agile environment. Since graduating I've been eagerly teaching myself new languages to build fun new things.
